# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

This document summarizes the key business findings derived from the sales analytics project built using **Google BigQuery & SQL**.


---

# Revenue Analysis

## Overview

| Metric               | Insight     |
| -------------------- | ----------- |
| Total Revenue        | **$29.3M+** |
| Best Performing Year | **2013**    |
| Strongest Quarter    | **Q4**      |

### Key Findings

* Revenue increased steadily over time.
* Q4 consistently outperformed other quarters.
* Sales patterns indicate healthy business growth.

> **Business Impact**
>
> Seasonal demand should be incorporated into inventory planning and marketing activities.

---

# Customer Insights

## Customer Value

### Observations

* Revenue is concentrated among a relatively small group of customers.
* Australia generates the highest revenue per customer.
* Purchase frequency alone is not a reliable indicator of customer value.

### Recommendation

* Build a VIP customer program.
* Prioritize high lifetime value customers.
* Personalize campaigns based on purchasing behavior.

---

# Product Performance

## Revenue Distribution

| Observation                        | Result          |
| ---------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Revenue generated by Bikes         | **96%+**        |
| Products generating 80% of revenue | **34 Products** |

### Key Findings

* Bike products dominate overall sales.
* Many products contribute little or no revenue.
* Revenue follows a strong Pareto distribution.

> **Business Impact**
>
> A small portion of the product catalog drives the majority of business revenue.

### Recommendation

* Prioritize high-performing products.
* Review low-selling products.
* Increase investment in emerging products.

---

# Sales Trends

## Monthly Performance

The company demonstrates a positive long-term sales trend with predictable seasonal fluctuations.

### Highlights

* Revenue accelerated significantly during 2013.
* Q4 consistently records the highest sales.
* Growth remains stable despite temporary fluctuations.

> **Business Impact**
>
> Forecasting models should include seasonality to improve planning accuracy.

---

# Pareto Analysis

## Revenue Concentration

The analysis confirms the **80/20 principle**.

| Metric                        | Result   |
| ----------------------------- | -------- |
| Products driving most revenue | **34**   |
| Revenue Contribution          | **~80%** |

### Recommendation

Focus inventory management and marketing investment on these strategic products.

---

# Customer Segmentation

Customers can be grouped according to their revenue contribution.

| Segment             | Description                          |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| VIP Customers       | High revenue and high lifetime value |
| Regular Customers   | Moderate purchasing activity         |
| Low Value Customers | Low revenue contribution             |

### Recommendation

Different customer segments require different retention and marketing strategies.

---

# Strategic Recommendations

## Product Strategy

* Prioritize high-performing bike models.
* Reduce investment in consistently underperforming products.
* Expand successful product lines into high-performing markets.

## Customer Strategy

* Launch loyalty programs for VIP customers.
* Improve customer retention initiatives.
* Personalize marketing campaigns.

## Sales Strategy

* Prepare inventory before Q4.
* Replicate successful campaigns from peak growth periods.
* Monitor customer lifetime value continuously.
